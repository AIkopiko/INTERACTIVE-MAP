About Us

Welcome to the Interactive Map Identifier!. This project aims to provide a user-friendly interface for navigating the CS and IT Building. This helps solve a common problem: people getting lost. The confusion and wasted time from trying to find a specific room can be stressful, especially for newcomers.

Our responsive design allows users to easily explore different floors, view important facilities, and access relevant information on any device. Our goal is to enhance the campus experience for students, faculty, and visitors.

The university is committed to modernization and uses technology to benefit the community, as demonstrated by this project. We ensure the information is accurate and the system is reliable. The map is adaptable to future changes it is designed to be easily updated as rooms and facilities change.

Thank you for using our Interactive Map Identifier. We hope it helps you navigate the campus with ease!
